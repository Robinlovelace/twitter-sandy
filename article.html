Descriptive statistics of hurricane tweets

The spatial distribution of Sandy tweets is heavily biased towards North America and Europe, with 80% originating in Northern America and 77% originating in the USA (Table x). It is interesting to note that the second most common country of origin is the UK, which is not close to the USA geographically but which has a high uptake of Twitter (ref) and is close culturally to North America (ref).
